Welcome to Cultural Insights,

your go-to destination for exploring the fascinating interplay between culture, nature, and lifelong learning.



Hello, my name is Tatyana Garkavaya, and I am the creator, writer, and driving force behind this blog.


With over 20 years of experience as a content creator, a strong background as a front-end developer, and an unwavering passion for travel, birding and discovery, I bring a unique perspective to every story shared here.


At the Cultural Insights, I dive into topics that connect people with nature and celebrate cultural heritage.


From traditional wildlife holidays in Bulgaria to innovative self-guided birdwatching tours in Bulgaria, my work highlights transformative practices that inspire change.


My expertise also extends to uncovering hidden gems like birding in Burgas, bringing you closer to the heart of Bulgaria’s extraordinary biodiversity and vibrant culture.


It's because I live in Burgas, on the second largest bird migratory route in Europe Via Pontica. So, most of my stories are inspired by birdwatching in Burgas and beyond.


Explore Three Captivating Sections of the Cultural Insights:


  • Trips: Stories of travel and conservation, from dolphin monitoring to collaborative efforts in protecting natural sanctuaries.

  • Interviews: Fascinating conversations with changemakers who are making a difference.

  • Recipes: Culinary traditions that tell the story of local cultures and sustainable practices.

Share Ideas & Inspire Changes



The blog’s motto, “Share Ideas & Inspire Change,” embodies my belief in the power of knowledge exchange.


With every blog post, I aim to inspire others to embrace sustainable living and explore innovative ideas that might otherwise go unnoticed.


The Cultural Insights logo—a man launching a kite — captures this spirit of discovery and joy. It’s inspired by one of my favorite volunteering experiences in Pomorie, Bulgaria, where a discarded kite became a symbol of hope during a beach cleanup.


I'll keep that fascination all my life. Every post I create is like launching that kite again, offering fresh perspectives and promoting solutions to today’s cultural and ecological challenges.


Promoting Sustainable Travel & Birding



The Cultural Insights has become an award-winning blog and recognized platform for promoting sustainable practices in art, travel, and conservation since its inception in 2020.


Two cornerstone projects shaped the blog’s mission:


  1. My master’s thesis on city branding, which explored how culture, nature and business intersect.

  2. A two-year ethnographic study, Mapping Sustainable Holidays in Bulgaria, which led to numerous accolades.


The blog’s most celebrated achievement is my award-winning book: Insights into Conservation Volunteer Bird Watching Holidays in Bulgaria: It's Not Only About Birds.


This guide is an essential resource for nature enthusiasts, offering self-guided tours and insider tips based on citizen science.
