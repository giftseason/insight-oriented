Sand + seawater = art



Looking for free things to do in Burgas? This seaside city isn’t just about sunny beaches and coastal charm—it’s also home to one of the most inspiring and creative activities for locals and visitors alike: sand art.



Every weekend, Burgas transforms into a hub of artistic innovation, thanks to the free sand sculpture workshops hosted by world-renowned sand artists Remy and Paul Hoggard from the Netherland.


With over 20 years of experience, this talented duo has created thousands of sand sculptures for festivals, galleries, and special events across Europe, the Americas, Asia, and beyond.



On the Burgas beach, they bring their expertise back home, inviting people of all ages and skill levels to unleash their creativity.


From massive castles to intricate mermaids, every workshop is a testament to how art can connect us with nature—and each other.


The key feature of the last weekend's event was a seductive belly mermaid surrounded by dolphins. According to the author of the composition Remy, the message to all women is: 'Enjoy yourself as you are in the new season, forget about standards and stereotypes'.


Free fun in Burgas for kids, adults, and families



Sand art is a universal language. Whether you're a 3-year-old building your first sandcastle or an adult exploring a creative outlet, this activity is perfect for everyone.


In fact, therapists recommend sand sculpting as a therapeutic exercise, offering a wide range of mental health benefits.


4 psychological benefits of sand art


  1. Stress Relief: The repetitive motions of working with sand calm the mind and body.

  2. Emotional Expression: Sand sculpting creates a safe space to process and express emotions.

  3. Conflict Resolution: Artistic exploration can help participants gain clarity on internal struggles.

  4. Mindful Creativity: Focusing on a project fosters mindfulness and a sense of achievement.

For families, these workshops are an especially delightful way to bond. Building a sand sculpture together fosters teamwork and communication while creating memories that last long after the tide washes away your masterpiece.


Art with a message



One of the most memorable creations from this season’s workshops was a breathtaking mermaid surrounded by dolphins, crafted by Remy herself.


The piece carried an empowering message for women: “Enjoy yourself as you are; forget about standards and stereotypes.”


This underlying blend of art and meaningful expression is what makes these workshops truly unique.


For those seeking free beach activities in Burgas, these workshops are not only fun but also deeply inspiring.


Try crafting a detailed face, a whimsical animal, or a towering castle, there are no limits—except your imagination.


With guidance from Remy and Paul, you’ll have the chance to create something that’s entirely your own or collaborate on collective projects.


Want to keep the creativity flowing? Follow the artists on Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok to explore their other projects and learn more about their incredible journey.


Plan Your Visit!


Don’t miss your chance to experience this one-of-a-kind initiative.


The free sand sculpture workshops take place every Saturday and Sunday from 10:00 to 12:00 on Burgas Beach near the Baywatch towers.


Bring your friends, family, and plenty of enthusiasm!


While the workshops are free, donations are appreciated to help support this inspiring project.


If you can’t contribute financially, a simple share on social media goes a long way in spreading the word.
